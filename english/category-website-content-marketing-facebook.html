BBC World News on Facebook - category Social/Social Content and Marketing/News & Information

BBC World News on Facebook serves as a window to the world for our BBC World News television operations. We deliver 24-hour breaking news, features, behind-the-scenes photos, commentary and expert analysis that not only drives engagement with our rapidly growing audience but informs the way we report the news on television and online. Our team of producers working in London, Singapore and Washington are encouraged to ask questions of our audience, challenge traditional forms of newsgathering and lead editorial innovation.
