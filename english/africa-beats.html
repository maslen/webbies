BBC Africa Beats - category Online Film and Video / General Film Categories / Music

BBC Africa Beats brings some of the best emerging musical talent from Africa to a worldwide online audience.

These intimate acoustic sessions recorded in Africa and London are exclusive to the BBC and represent a unique opportunity to follow the African music scene, its new faces and trends.

The series reflects the diversity of a huge continent, from Malawi to Nigeria, and it combines the fresh sound of up and coming African artists with personal insightful comments, shedding light on the cultures the musicians come from.
