See the world's greatest cities through the eyes of BBC World Service reporters who lived there: My City series opens a door to what life is really like in their home city.

From Ho Chi Minh's scooter club to the boxing suburb of Bukom in Accra, the films convey the vivid sights and sounds of each place.

As well as being published on www.bbc.com/travel and shown on BBC World TV, the series was translated and published on the BBC's many language websites, such as BBC Russian.
